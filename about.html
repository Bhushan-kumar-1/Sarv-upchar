Sarv-Upchar offers a multi-role registration system that supports patients, doctors, and hospital staff. This makes the platform reliable and easy to use for everyone involved in the healthcare process. Patients can register to check their appointment time, see how many people are waiting, and know exactly when they will meet the doctor. Hospital staff can use the system to schedule appointments, assign doctors, and manage available time slots. Doctors can log in to view all upcoming appointments and track the number of patients waiting. This multi-user functionality ensures smooth coordination, reduces waiting time, and improves overall hospital management.
